About Him

Allu Arjun is an Indian film actor who primarily works in Telugu cinema.[1] After playing as a child artist in Vijetha and as a dancer in Daddy, Arjun made his adult debut in Gangotri.[2][3] Arjun then appeared in Sukumar's debut film Arya.[4][5] His role in Arya was his breakthrough, earning him his first Filmfare Best Telugu Actor Award nomination and he won a Special Jury award at the Nandi Awards ceremony, two CineMAA Awards for Best Actor and Best Actor Jury[6] and the film was a critical and commercial success.He next starred in V. V. Vinayak's Bunny playing the role of Bunny, a college student. Critics praised his mannerisms and dancing.[8] His next film was A. Karunakaran's musical love story Happy.
